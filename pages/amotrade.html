Agriculture

Through Modern Agriculture

AmoTrade carries out the wholesale of over 80,000 tons of fruits and vegetables, cooperating with more than 30 countries in both the Azerbaijani and international markets.

Fruit and vegetable farming is a branch of agriculture focused on growing, cultivating, and preparing various types of fruits and vegetables for sale. This type of farming makes a significant contribution to the production of healthy food and the supply of both local and international markets.

Advantages:
A natural and healthy food source Access to local and global markets Support for economic development and employment
